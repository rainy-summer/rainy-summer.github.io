About Us

Our products are crafted with love, and use the highest quality ingredients.

The Name

Our name is in reference to the Lotus-eaters from antiquity. We find the indulgent idealism depicted in those stories to be revolutionary. Live and let live, free from pressures and the displeasures of modern society.

All of our products use natural ingredients, are locally made in Alberta, and tell a unique story.

Beautifully Natural

Not 97%, not 99%, 100% natural. When we say natural, we actually mean it. We prioritize pure and clean ingredients above all else. We believe in nature and its ability to soothe ailments, calm the mind and invoke feelings of joy—that’s why we make plant-based products to power every aspect of your life.

Healthy Habits

We believe holistic wellness is the result of small, impactful choices made every day. That’s why we offer all-natural solutions that seamlessly integrate into your life and a community to support you in feeling your best, every step of the way.
